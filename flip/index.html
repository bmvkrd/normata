Welcome

Flashcards are a simple yet powerful tool for mastering new information. They promote active recall, which is far more effective than passive review. When combined with spaced repetition, flashcards help the brain store information more reliably by revisiting it at optimal intervals. This scientifically-backed approach improves retention and reduces study time.

FLIP leverages these techniques by making it easy to create, organize, and review study sets with intelligent scheduling. The app tracks what you know and what you don’t, helping you focus where it matters most.

Create or Download Study Sets

Easily create your own study sets tailored to your needs or download from our curated catalog of pre-built sets. Whether you're preparing for exams or learning a language, FLIP helps you get started faster.

Card Groups

With Card Groups, you can break down big sets into smaller, themed sections. It’s a smart way to master complex topics step by step.

Answer Choices

Simplify study set creation by using predefined answer options. Great for topics with repetitive or categorical answers.
