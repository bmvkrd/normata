What is Flip?

Flip is an educational flash card app designed to support learners of all ages with memorization and long-term retention. Using proven techniques like spaced repetition, Flip transforms studying into an interactive and effective experience.

Key Features

  • Interactive Learning: Memorize effectively through a dynamic and engaging study process.
  • Rich Flash Cards: Create cards with a question on the front, an answer on the back, and optional additional context on both sides.
  • Study Set Groups: Break large study sets into smaller groups of 10 to 100 cards, making learning more manageable and less overwhelming.
  • Bulk Card Importer: Quickly create study sets by uploading cards in bulk via CSV files.
  • Catalog of Study Sets: Browse a growing collection of curated Study Sets across a variety of categories, designed to help learners quickly get started and accelerate their progress.
  • Ad-Free Experience: Focus entirely on learning without distractions or interruptions.
