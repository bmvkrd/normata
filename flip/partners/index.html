How it works

Flip helps teachers and language schools make learning more effective and engaging — whether it’s vocabulary, historical facts, grammar rules, or science terms.

With Flip, you can easily create your own flashcard study sets and share them with students using unique access codes. Students simply enter the code in the Flip app to start learning — no registration required, and completely free.

Whether you teach online or in the classroom, Flip gives your students a simple and motivating way to review and remember what they’ve learned.
