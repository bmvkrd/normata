Flip

Your companion for learning, reviewing, and remembering with cards.

Master anything with smart, customizable card-based learning.

Flip is your personal study companion designed to help you learn efficiently and stay on track — whether you're preparing for exams, brushing up on vocabulary, or exploring a new subject.

With Flip, you can create or download Study Sets, each containing cards with content on the front and back. Think of each card as a bite-sized piece of knowledge you can review, memorize, and master at your own pace.

Why You'll Love Flip

  • Create or Download Study Sets: Build your own or explore our growing catalog of ready-made sets.
  • Card Groups – Learn in Chunks: Split large sets into focused batches to avoid overload.
  • Track Your Progress: Monitor mastery across sets, groups, and individual cards.
  • Adaptive Learning Mode: Engage in multiple choice and input rounds to strengthen memory.
  • Bulk Card Importing: Import large sets easily to save time.
  • Deep Customization: Tailor your experience from card groups to quiz settings.
